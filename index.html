File formats

JPG

jpg = photography, images not requiring transparnecy and Anything needing color fidelity (gradients).

GIF

gif = small animations (think internet memes)icons (if not reproducible with icon fonts) Areas of continuous color (no gradients)

PNG

png = images with lots of transparency,logos, icons, etc.Saved in 24-bit for best results.

SVG

SVG = Scaleable vector graphics
